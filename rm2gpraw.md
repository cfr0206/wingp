rm2gpraw (Jul 13 2010)
Utilite to convert RedMonEE multipage tiff to gpraw format.
Usage:
rm2gpraw [--help | -h]
rm2gpraw {--image | -i}<filename.tiff> [--log | -l] [--flipv | -v] [--fliph | -g]
rm2gpraw {--image | -i}<filename.tiff> [-lvg]

Options:
--help,  -h	- help
--image, -i	- input multipage tiff file
--log,   -l	- enable log
--flipv, -v	- flip vertical
--fliph, -g	- flip horizontal
